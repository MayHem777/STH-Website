## The Space Your Home Is Missing

Every home needs a proper utility space — somewhere for the laundry, the tools, the hobby gear, the overflow storage. A purpose-built utility room does this properly, without the compromise of a prefab shed from the hardware store.

We build utility rooms the same way we build our tiny houses and modular homes — properly framed, properly clad, and finished to last. The result is a space that looks like it belongs on your property and will still be standing in 30 years.

### Laundry Rooms

Free up space inside your home by moving the laundry out back. We can fit a full laundry — trough, washing machine and dryer provisions, hot and cold plumbing, cabinetry, and a folding bench. Plumbed and connected, ready to use.

### Workshops & Hobby Rooms

A proper workshop needs proper power. We wire utility rooms with 15A outlets, RCD protection, and adequate lighting for detailed work. Heavy-duty flooring — polished concrete or epoxy — handles tools, spills and heavy equipment. Wide access doors accommodate vehicles, machinery or large materials.

We can also build out art studios, music rooms, or photography studios — insulated, climate-controlled, and fit for purpose.

### Storage

Need a secure, weatherproof space for bikes, kayaks, garden equipment, or long-term storage? We build utility rooms with wide roller doors, shelving systems, and insulation options to protect temperature-sensitive contents.

### Approval

Utility rooms are Class 10a non-habitable structures, placing them in the same approval category as garages and sheds. In most Queensland councils, a compliant structure within standard size and setback limits is accepted development — no development application required.

We'll confirm the approval pathway for your specific property when you enquire.
