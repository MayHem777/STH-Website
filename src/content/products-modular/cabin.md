## Cabin

Our cabin range is designed for those who want a beautiful, self-contained space without the footprint of a full home. Whether it's a guest retreat, a short-stay rental, or a compact permanent home, there's a cabin model to suit.

### Banksia
A cosy studio/bedroom cabin designed to address housing shortages and provide extra residential space for family and guests. Available with two roof options.

### Dahlia
Everything you need in one beautifully designed space — a cosy living/sleeping area, stylish kitchen, and spa-like bathroom, all opening to a covered deck. Perfect for Airbnb, caravan parks, or a guest retreat.

### Raglan
Sleek architectural lines, warm timber finishes, and large windows that connect with nature. Features a plush queen bed, lounge area, work nook, and a luxurious bathroom with rain shower and freestanding bath.
