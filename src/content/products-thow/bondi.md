## Bondi

The Bondi is designed for those who love the indoors but can't get enough of the outdoors. At 7.2 metres, there's room to breathe — and with large windows and a thoughtful layout, the outside world is always close.

A step up from our 6m models, the Bondi gives you more living space without sacrificing road-readiness. Perfect for couples, short-stay investment, or anyone who wants a beautiful base to come home to.

### Inclusions

Custom triple axle trailer (4.5T ATM), Bluescope steel frame, Colorbond trimdeck roof, cedar feature cladding, full kitchen with Westinghouse appliances, Mitsubishi split-system air conditioning, Rinnai instantaneous gas hot water, and semi-frameless bathroom with white subway tile sheeting.

Optional upgrades include solar systems, home automation, skylights, and single-drawer dishwasher.

### Build Levels

- **Lock-Up** — Structure, cladding, roof, windows and doors installed. From $71,500 incl. GST.
- **Shell** — Lock-Up plus electrical and plumbing rough-in. Price on application.
- **Turn Key** — Fully finished and move-in ready. From $135,000 incl. GST.

*Note: Toilet not included. Options range from $400 to $8,000+.*
