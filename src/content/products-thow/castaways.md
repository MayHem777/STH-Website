## Castaways

The Castaways is where simplicity meets luxury. Same footprint as the Granite Bay at 8 metres, but with premium finishes that set it apart — including a stunning cedar shiplap ceiling that runs the full length of the home.

With dual sleeping lofts for four and a beautifully designed living area below, the Castaways is just as comfortable as a permanent home — just with the freedom to move.

### Inclusions

Custom triple axle trailer with 4 jack legs and electric brakes (4.5T ATM), Bluescope steel frame, Colorbond roof, cedar shiplap ceiling feature, full kitchen with Westinghouse appliances, Mitsubishi split-system air conditioning, Rinnai B16 instantaneous gas hot water, and polymarble bathroom with semi-frameless screen.

### Build Levels

- **Lock-Up** — Structure, cladding, roof, windows and doors installed. From $71,500 incl. GST.
- **Shell** — Lock-Up plus electrical and plumbing rough-in. Price on application.
- **Turn Key** — Fully finished and move-in ready. From $135,000 incl. GST.

*Note: Toilet not included. Options range from $400 to $8,000+.*
