## Built for Australian Roads

A tiny house is only as good as the trailer it sits on. Ours are engineered and manufactured specifically for tiny houses — not converted boat trailers or repurposed stock crates. Every trailer is designed to carry the full weight of a completed home, handle Queensland's roads, and last the life of the build.

We use hot-dip galvanised steel throughout — not paint, not cold-dip. Hot-dip galvanising penetrates the steel rather than coating the surface, giving you corrosion protection that holds up in coastal Queensland conditions for decades.

### Engineered and Certified

Every trailer is engineer-certified for road registration in Queensland. You receive a full engineering certification package with your trailer, covering axle loads, hitch ratings, and braking compliance. No chasing paperwork or paying for independent certification later.

### Designed for Tiny Houses

Standard trailers aren't designed with tiny houses in mind. Our trailers have recessed wheel arches that don't intrude into the floor plan, outrigger extensions for wider build widths, and built-in jacking points so your home can be levelled on site without cradles or improvised setups.

### Available Separately

We sell trailers separately to other builders and owner-builders. If you're planning your own build and need a quality, certified starting point, contact us for pricing and lead times.
