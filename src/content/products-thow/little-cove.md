## Little Cove

The Little Cove proves that great things come in small packages. At 6.2 metres, this single-loft tiny house is thoughtfully designed to maximise every centimetre — bright, airy, and packed with clever storage.

Whether you're downsizing, looking for an investment short-stay, or chasing a simpler lifestyle, the Little Cove delivers a comfortable, sustainable home that's ready to go wherever you go.

### Inclusions

Custom-built dual or triple axle trailer, Bluescope steel frame, Colorbond trimdeck roof with R2.5 insulation and thermal break wall wrap, full kitchen with Westinghouse appliances, bathroom with polymarble shower, Mitsubishi split-system air conditioning, and Rinnai instantaneous gas hot water.

Interior linings are your choice: plywood, VJ sheeting, cedar, or pine — all finished with a 3-coat paint system.

### Build Levels

- **Lock-Up** — Structure, cladding, roof, windows and doors installed. From $60,000 incl. GST.
- **Shell** — Lock-Up plus electrical and plumbing rough-in. Price on application.
- **Turn Key** — Fully finished and move-in ready. From $115,000 incl. GST.

*Note: Toilet not included. Options range from $400 to $8,000+.*
